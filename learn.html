Learn Crystals

Every stone carries a story and an energy. Discover the meaning behind each crystal and choose the one that aligns with your intention.

Rose Quartz

Love · Compassion · Emotional Healing

Known as the stone of unconditional love, Rose Quartz opens the heart to compassion, self-love, and deep emotional healing. It invites gentle, nurturing energy into your relationships and your relationship with yourself.

Amethyst

Calm · Intuition · Spiritual Growth

A deeply calming stone that soothes an overactive mind, eases stress, and deepens intuition. Amethyst supports meditation, restful sleep, and spiritual awareness.

Clear Quartz

Clarity · Amplification · Protection

The master healer. Clear Quartz amplifies energy and intention, clears the mind, and brings balance and protection. It magnifies the energy of any crystal it is paired with.

Citrine

Abundance · Confidence · Joy

A radiant stone of abundance and positivity. Citrine encourages confidence, motivation, and warm, joyful energy — a natural companion for new beginnings and prosperity.

Aquamarine

Calm · Courage · Communication

Soothing as the sea, Aquamarine calms the emotions, encourages clear and honest communication, and brings courage in times of change.

Green Fluorite

Focus · Harmony · Renewal

Green Fluorite clears mental fog and restores balance. It supports focus, decision-making, and a sense of renewal and harmony.

Moonstone

New Beginnings · Balance · Intuition

A stone of new beginnings, Moonstone is deeply connected to feminine energy and intuition. It brings inner balance and gently guides you through change.

Black Tourmaline & Obsidian

Protection · Grounding · Strength

Powerful protective stones that ground your energy and shield against negativity. They bring stability, strength, and a sense of security.
